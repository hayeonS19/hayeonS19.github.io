What is Nebula?

Nebula is a powerful tool designed to enhance the convenience and accessibility of Cosmo/Discord users. It offers various features to streamline user interactions and improve their overall experience on Discord.

Key Features:

  • Send: Send your Objekt on Discord! You don't need to open cosmo app to send objkets anymore!
  • Trade History: View recent trades with detailed information about the sender, receiver, and cards involved.
  • Collect Tab: Easily access and manage your collection directly through Discord.
  • Profile Customization: Create and customize your profile to show your favorite objekt and info about you.
  • Rekord: Upload Rekord using Nebula

Nebula aims to provide a seamless and enhanced user experience for all Cosmo/Discord enthusiasts by integrating essential functionalities and making them accessible through a user-friendly features.
