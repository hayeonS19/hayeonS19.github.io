What is Nebula?

Nebula is a powerful tool designed to enhance the convenience and accessibility of Cosmo/Discord users.

Key Features:

  • /profile: Show your Cosmo profile includes seasonal objekt stats, como per month and also your recent vote history.
  • /collection: Show your objekt list; You can also search up specific objekt you are looking for.
  • /activity: Show your trade history.
  • /objekt_info: Search up specific objekt.
  • /connect: Connect your Cosmo account with Nebula so that you don't need to put your Cosmo ID every time using command.
  • /disconnect: Disconnect your Cosmo account with Nebula.
  • /nebula: Customize your Nebula profile.

Nebula aims to provide a seamless and enhanced user experience for all Cosmo/Discord enthusiasts by integrating essential functionalities and making them accessible through user-friendly features.
