What is Nebula?

Nebula is a powerful tool designed to enhance the convenience and accessibility of Cosmo/Discord users. It offers various features to streamline user interactions and improve their overall experience on Discord.

Key Features:

  • /profile: Show your cosmo profile includes seasonal objekt stats, como per month and also your recent vote history.
  • /collection: Show your objekt list; You can also search up specific objekt you are looking for.
  • /activity: Show your trade history.
  • /objekt_info: Search up specific objekt.
  • /connect: Connect your cosmo account with Nebula so that you don't need to put your cosmo ID everytime using command; non-login option
  • /disconnect: Disconnect your cosmo account with Nebula.
  • /nebula: Customize your nebula profile.

Nebula aims to provide a seamless and enhanced user experience for all Cosmo/Discord enthusiasts by integrating essential functionalities and making them accessible through a user-friendly features.
